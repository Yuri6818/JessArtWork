About Me

Welcome to PetalFawnStudio! I'm a professional digital artist specializing in custom character designs, adoptables, and base creations. With years of experience and a passion for bringing characters to life, I work closely with clients to create unique, high-quality artwork.

Every piece is crafted with attention to detail and love for the art form. Whether you're looking for a custom commission, a YCH slot, or a ready-made adopt, I'm here to help bring your vision to reality.

Commission Process

1. Initial Contact: To begin, contact me with your commission request, including character references and a description of your idea. We will discuss the details and I will provide a price quote.

2. Sketch & Approval: Once payment is made, I will create a preliminary sketch for your review. You may request major changes at this stage. Up to 3 rounds of minor revisions are included.

3. Final Artwork: After the sketch is approved, I will proceed with lining and coloring the artwork. Only minor color adjustments can be made at this stage. Turnaround time is typically 1-2 weeks.

Usage Rights

Personal use is permitted for all commissioned work. You may post the artwork on your social media (with credit), use it as a profile picture, etc. Commercial use requires a separate licensing agreement and fee. You may not resell, redistribute, or claim my artwork as your own creation.

Payment & Refunds

Payment is required upfront for all work via PayPal. Refunds are available before work begins (minus any transaction fees). Once work has started, refunds are provided on a case-by-case basis depending on the amount of work completed.

Artist Rights

I, the artist, retain the copyright to the artwork and the right to display it in my portfolio, on social media, and for promotional purposes. I reserve the right to refuse any commission request for any reason. The client will not be charged if the commission is refused.
