Bases for Sale

Character bases are pre-drawn templates that you can customize and use as a starting point for your own art. Each base pack includes both PSD (Photoshop) and PNG files.

What you get:

  • PSD file: Editable Photoshop file with layers for easy customization
  • PNG file: High-quality image file ready to download and use immediately

Purchase flow: Click a base to purchase. After purchase, the files will be sent to the email address associated with your PayPal account for instant download.
